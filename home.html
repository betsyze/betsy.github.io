Betsy Ellison (b. 1999) is an interdisciplinary designer, artist, and researcher, currently working from Chicago, IL. Currently stationed at FCB Chicago as a UX Architect, her clients have included Discover Card and Bank, the Internet Archive, and various local non-profits and micro-publishers. She maintains an independent practice across digital media, design, and contemporary art, and is currently accepting freelance clients.
